UNSW Millennium Sundial

In 1999, Prof. Bill Kearsley and Dr. George Bennett convinced the Vice Chancellor to build a sundial on the South Tower of the Quadrangle Building.

Kearsely and Bennett’s vertically-mounted sundial catches morning and afternoon sun. The roman numerals are the hourly indicators. The small lines in-between are the half hour indicators. The sundial’s shadow changes in elevation throughout the seasons.
