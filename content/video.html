Arc @ UNSW

Arc is UNSW’s student organisation. Their mission is to make your life at Uni more interesting, more fun, more rewarding and more affordable! It’s their aim to give you the best possible student experience while you’re here with us.

The Arc office is also where you’ll find the Blitz team, it’s our student magazine and guide to what’s happening on campus and around Sydney. Membership is FREE to Arc - sign up and enjoy the ride!
