Renewable energy at the Quad

UNSW was the first university in Australia to generate solar power on campus and we did it right here at the Quad. In 2005, we installed a 42kWp photovoltaic array (solar panels) on the roof of the Quadrangle Building. The electricity produced is used throughout the building.

Since 2005, we have added 10kW panels to the Fitness and Aquatic Centre, 18kW panels on the childcare centres and a 146kW system on top of the Tyree Energy Technology Building.
