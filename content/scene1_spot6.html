Facilities and the Quad Food Court

The Quad is one of the busiest spots on campus. At this location you’ll find a food court, bookstore, bank and ATM, convenience store and a travel agent.

The Quad Food Court is a central eating area with indoor and outdoor tables. Spread across two levels, you can take your pick from sushi, Thai, burgers, kebabs, noodles, café sandwiches and more.

Outdoor tables have built-in power sockets so you can charge your phone or laptop, or keep studying or working while you eat. It is also a great place to socialise and randomly run into friends.
